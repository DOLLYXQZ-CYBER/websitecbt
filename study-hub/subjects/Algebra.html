What is Algebra?

Algebra is a branch of mathematics that uses letters and symbols to represent numbers. Example:

x + 5 = 10

To find x: 10 - 5 = 5 Therefore, x = 5

Worked Example

Solve: 2x + 4 = 12

2x = 12 - 4

2x = 8

x = 4

About TobilobaTech Academy

TobilobaTech Academy is an online learning platform created to help students learn web development and prepare for Nigerian examinations such as JAMB and WAEC.

Our Mission

🎯 Empower Students

We aim to make quality coding education and exam preparation accessible to everyone.

💻 Practical Learning

Students learn by practicing, building projects and taking CBT tests.

🚀 Build the Future

Our goal is to help students gain skills that can lead to careers in technology.

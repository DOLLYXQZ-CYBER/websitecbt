What is an HTML Form?

HTML forms allow users to enter information such as names, email addresses, passwords, phone numbers and more. 

<form>

  <input type="text" placeholder="Your Name">

  <input type="email" placeholder="Email">

  <input type="password" placeholder="Password">

  <button>Submit</button>

</form>

Explanation

  • <form> creates the form.
  • <input> collects user information.
  • type="text" is for names or text.
  • type="email" accepts email addresses.
  • type="password" hides passwords.
  • <button> submits the form.

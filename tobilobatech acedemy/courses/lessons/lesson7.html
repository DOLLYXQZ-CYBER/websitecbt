🖼 Lesson 7 - Images, Audio & Video

💎 Premium Lesson • Learn how to add multimedia to your website.

Adding an Image

<img src="image.jpg" alt="My Image" width="300">
💡 Tip: Always add the alt attribute. It improves accessibility and SEO.

Adding Audio

<audio controls>

<source src="music.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">

</audio>

Adding Video

<video controls width="400">

<source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">

</video>
⚠ Common Mistake: Large image and video files can make your website slow. Compress them before uploading.
